Goodbye, Hackintosh!

Aug 20, 2024

After 7 (or 8, or 10, I don’t remember exactly) years, I’ve parted ways with hackintosh. It’s because Apple’s M chips are more than enough for all my work and video/photo editing needs.

I have to admit that this has been true since M1; but, I had always kept the hackintosh tower with more RAM and a more powerful graphics card around, and convinced myself it would save my ass some day.

But, the day has never came…

In fact, I hadn’t even turn the hackintosh tower on for more than two months before converting it to a Windows gaming machine last night.

(Yea, Windows, I know…)

Anyway, it was hell of a ride, and the journey has came to an end.

Farewell and goodbye, Hackintosh!

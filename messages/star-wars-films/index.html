I’ve started to watch Star Wars films from the beginning. I initially was afraid that it would be hard to bear because older movies are very slow, but it turned out to be the other way around. I was hooked on the story after the first 15 minutes and couldn’t help but finished the whole Episode IV in one run. It’s not like other recent films which I need to break one up to smaller pieces to consume. This is very interesting! Onto the Episode V now!
